About Me

Guided by the cosmos

Valkyrie No Jutsu, "The Art of the Valkyries"

In mythology, Valkyries were divine emissaries, winged guardians who moved freely between the world of the living and the realm beyond. They did not judge; they witnessed. They held the sacred space between worlds with grace, strength, and unwavering clarity, guiding souls along their passage.

That is the essence of this work. As your astrologer, I serve as a bridge between who you are and who your soul came here to become, between the life you are living and the cosmic blueprint written in your chart at the moment of your birth. The stars do not dictate your fate. They illuminate your path.

Astrology found me when religion and conventional paths left my deepest questions unanswered. Despite building a seemingly beautiful life, I felt a quiet, persistent emptiness. My turning point came through 3 major health scares that made me rethink what really matters. Forced back to the basics, my physical healing journey unlocked a deep connection to energy, guiding me eventually to astrology, the ultimate map for my soul.

I have navigated significant, life-altering transits: Saturn Return, Sade Sati, and a Pluto-Pluto square. These experiences, along with my deep study, allow me to hold space for your own karmic journey with true compassion.

I look forward to working with you.

Agreements · Please Read Before Booking

To honor our sacred exchange, I maintain very firm boundaries that protect both our time and energy.

Late Changes (<48 Hours)

Chart preparation is already underway, making all session fees non-refundable. If you have an unexpected life event, you are permitted one late reschedule within this window. Subsequent changes forfeit the fee and require a new booking.

No-Shows

A 10-minute grace period is provided at the start of the call. Missing this window counts as a missed appointment and forfeits the fee. True emergencies are exempted, but otherwise you must rebook.

My work empowers your inner knowing rather than dictating your path. I offer spiritual insight instead of predictions. You retain full responsibility for your choices and your free will.

To maintain a mutually respectful environment, I reserve the right to refuse service or decline future bookings at my sole discretion at any time.

By proceeding with your booking and payment, you automatically acknowledge and agree to all terms outlined above.
