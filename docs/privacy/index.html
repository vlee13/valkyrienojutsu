Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what information Valkyrie No Jutsu ("we", "I") collects and how it is used.

Information collected

  • Booking details: When you book a reading, scheduling and contact information (name, email, birth details you choose to share) are collected through Calendly.
  • Payment: Payments are processed by third-party providers (Calendly, PayPal). We never see or store your full payment card details.
  • Email: If you email us, we receive the contents of your message.

How it is used

Information is used solely to schedule and deliver your reading, respond to your inquiries, and process payment. We do not sell your information.

Third-party services

This site uses Calendly (scheduling/payment), PayPal (payment), and Linktree (link hub). Each has its own privacy policy governing data they collect.
